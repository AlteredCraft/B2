The habits that set up a good night: going to bed and getting up at the same
hour every day, keeping the bedroom dark, quiet, and slightly cool, and winding
down with a screen-free routine — reading, stretching, dim light — for the last
half hour. The bed is reserved for sleeping, so the mind learns that lying down
means switching off rather than scrolling or working.
