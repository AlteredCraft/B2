A squeaking drivetrain is a dry one: degrease the chain, re-lube it, and wipe
the excess so grit doesn't stick. Sloppy or skipping gear changes usually trace
to cable tension — a quarter turn of the barrel adjuster at the derailleur
trues up the indexing. Keep tires at the pressure printed on the sidewall and
replace brake pads before the wear groove disappears.
