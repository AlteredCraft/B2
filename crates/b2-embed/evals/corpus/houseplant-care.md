Most indoor plants die of kindness: overwatering drowns the pot, so let the top
of the soil dry before watering again and always use a container that drains.
Place ferns and other tropicals away from radiators and direct sun — bright,
indirect light and a bit of humidity keep fronds from crisping and browning at
the tips. Yellowing lower leaves usually mean soggy soil, not thirst.
