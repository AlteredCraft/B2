Length beats cleverness: a long passphrase of several random words holds up
better than a short string of symbol substitutions, because guessing cost grows
with every added character. Never reuse the same credential across sites — one
breach then opens all of them — and keep each unique one in a manager rather
than in memory. Avoid birthdays, pet names, and anything a stranger could
scrape from a profile.
