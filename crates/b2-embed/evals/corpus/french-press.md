An immersion brewer: coarse grounds steep directly in hot water for about four
minutes, then a metal screen on a plunger is pushed slowly to the bottom of the
carafe to hold the spent grounds back while you pour. Because nothing paper
filters the liquid, the oils stay in and the cup drinks heavy and full-bodied,
with a little fine sediment settling in the last sip.
