A fraud email dressed up as your bank, a courier, or the IT desk, engineered to
panic you into clicking: an urgent subject line, a spoofed sender domain one
letter off from the real one, and a link to a fake login page that harvests
whatever you type. The defenses are boring and effective — check the actual
address, hover before clicking, and when a message demands you "verify your
account", go to the site directly instead.
