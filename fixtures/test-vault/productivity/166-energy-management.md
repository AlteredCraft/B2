# Energy Management

Notes on energy management within the broader theme of productivity.

## Default to Action

Protect a block of uninterrupted time before the meetings colonize your calendar. A weekly review is where scattered inputs get processed into next actions. Timeboxing assigns a fixed budget to a task so it cannot expand to fill the day. A clear next action removes the friction that makes a task easy to avoid.

Deep work is the ability to focus without distraction on a cognitively demanding task. If a task takes under two minutes, do it now rather than tracking it. A clear next action removes the friction that makes a task easy to avoid. Match your hardest work to the hours when your energy is highest.
