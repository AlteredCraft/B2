# Single-Origin Beans

Notes on single-origin beans within the broader theme of coffee brewing.

## Grind Size

Freshly ground beans lose aromatics within minutes, so grind right before brewing. Beans are best a week or two off roast, once they have degassed but not gone stale. Dialing in means changing one variable at a time and tasting the result. Grind size is the main dial: finer extracts faster, coarser slows the flow. See [[coffee/108-extraction-yield]]. See [[coffee/088-brew-ratio]].
