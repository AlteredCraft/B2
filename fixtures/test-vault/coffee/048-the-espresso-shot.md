# The Espresso Shot

Notes on the espresso shot within the broader theme of coffee brewing.

## Extraction Yield

Extraction yield is the fraction of the coffee's mass that ends up dissolved in the cup. A brew ratio around one to sixteen is a reliable starting point to tune from. Under-extraction tastes sour and thin; over-extraction tastes bitter and hollow. Water just off the boil, around ninety-three degrees, extracts without scorching.
